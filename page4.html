Let's look at our Sankey Diagram!

Take Away:

This Sankey diagram shows how songs from the most common genres flow into either the clean or explicit category. It highlights which genres are more strongly associated with explicit content and which are mostly clean. This makes it easier to understand how lyrical content differs across genres at a glance.

Interaction:

Hover over the flows or nodes to see more detailed values for each connection. The width of each flow represents the number of songs in that category, so wider links mean a stronger connection between a genre and either clean or explicit content.
