Tree Map: Popularity Distribution Across Genres

Take Away:

This treemap shows how popularity is distributed across the top Spotify genres, broken into three tiers: Low (0–20), Medium (20–50), and High (50–100). The size of each block represents how many songs fall into that tier, making it easy to compare which genres produce more highly-rated tracks versus ones that tend to fly under the radar.

Interaction:

Hover over any block to see the genre, popularity tier, and exact number of songs in that segment. The size of each colored section within a genre tells you how that genre's songs are spread across popularity levels. For example, a larger green block means more highly popular songs relative to the others.
