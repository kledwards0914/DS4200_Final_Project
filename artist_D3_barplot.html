D3 Bar Plot: Top Spotify Artists by Number of Songs

Take Away:

This bar chart shows which artists appear most often in our Spotify dataset by comparing the number of songs associated with each top artist. It makes it easy to see that a small number of artists show up much more frequently than others, which suggests the dataset is concentrated around certain artists rather than being evenly spread out. This helps highlight which artists are most represented in the sample.

Interaction:

Use the sorting buttons to reorder the bars by song count or alphabetically by artist name. This makes it easier to compare artists in different ways depending on whether the focus is popularity in the dataset or finding a specific artist. Use the dropdown menu to change how many top artists are displayed, and hover over each bar to see the exact number of songs for that artist.
