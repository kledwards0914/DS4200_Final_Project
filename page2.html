Histogram: The Effects of Energy

Take Away:

TFor this histogram, we used a sample of 2,500 songs to keep the visualization clear and not too crowded. We also focused on the top 5 genres by frequency since the dataset includes over 100 genres, and showing all of them would make the chart hard to read. The histogram shows how often songs fall into different energy ranges, making it easier to compare which genres tend to have lower or higher energy songs. This helps highlight differences in the overall feel and intensity of music across genres.

Interaction:

Click a genre in the legend to filter the visualization so only that genre is shown. You can also drag across the histogram to select a specific energy range. The linked chart below updates based on that selection, making it easier to explore how popularity changes within certain energy levels.
