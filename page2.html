Let's look at our Histogram!

Take Away:

This histogram compares the distribution of energy scores across the top five genres. It shows how frequently songs fall into different energy ranges, making it easier to compare which genres tend to have lower-energy versus higher-energy songs. This helps reveal differences in the overall feel and intensity of music across genres.

Interaction:

Click a genre in the legend to highlight that genre and fade the others. This makes it easier to focus on one genre’s energy distribution without losing the full comparison. Hover over the bars to see the count of songs in each energy range.
