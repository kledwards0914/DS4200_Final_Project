Let's look at our Scatter Plot!

Take Away:

This scatter plot explores the relationship between tempo and danceability for songs by the most common artists across major genres. It helps show whether faster songs are always more danceable and whether certain artists or genres cluster together. The chart suggests that danceability does not depend only on tempo, since songs with similar tempos can still have very different danceability scores.

Interaction:

Use the artist dropdown menu to filter the chart by a specific artist, or leave it on “All” to view every song. Hover over any point to see the artist, tempo, and danceability values. When an artist is selected, their songs stay highlighted while the others fade into the background.
