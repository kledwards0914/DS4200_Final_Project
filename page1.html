Let's look at our boxplot!

Take Away:

This boxplot compares how popularity scores vary across the top Spotify genres. It helps show which genres tend to have higher typical popularity, which ones are more spread out, and where there are outliers. The chart makes it easy to see that some genres are consistently more popular, while others have a wider range of song performance.

Interaction:

To read this visualization, compare the median line inside each box, the overall height of the box, and any outlier points. The median shows the typical popularity for that genre, while the spread shows how much popularity varies within it. Hover over the box plots to see specific statistics such as the max, Q3, median, Q1, and miniumm of the genre that plot represents.
